## 📐 Image Optimization Specs
For optimal loading performance:
- **Hero Headshot:** ~600x800px, WebP format preferred, max 200KB.
- **Case Study Covers:** ~1200x600px, WebP or high-compression JPG, max 300KB.
- **Avatars:** 120x120px, JPG/PNG, max 50KB.
- **Logos:** SVG format highly recommended for crisp scaling.
